Project: Pigeon

TypeScript, OOP

About

Project: Pigeon is a rogue-like webgame where you play as a professor who needs to capture and cure pigeons.
There are 3 levels and 2 bossfights. The game gets harder as it goes on.
It is based on 3 keywords: Narrative, explore and pigeons.
The narrative aspect of the game could've gotten a bit more love, but the pigeons are definitely there.
The game was made in a 7 week period by a team of 5 students.

My work

I helped coming up with the concept, drew the pigeons, professor, final boss, final level background and poster.
Besides that I did a lot of the coding, like the attack and health mechanics, pigeons movement, the linking of all the rooms and the start- and end screens.

Technology

Everything is coded in 'vanilla' TypeScript and a bit of HTML/CSS. Using just regular TS probably wasn't the easiest way to build a webgame, but it worked.
Art assets are made with Adobe Photoshop.
